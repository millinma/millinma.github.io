I am a post-doctoral researcher and teacher at the [Chair of Health Informatics](https://kiinformatik.mri.tum.de/en/chair-health-informatics-chi) ([Technical University of Munich](https://www.tum.de/en/) [Hospital](https://mri.tum.de/de)) under the guidance of Prof. [Björn Schuller](http://schuller.one), focusing on fundamentals and applications of deep learning (DL). Due to my background in both physics and computer science, I try to better understand what is going on in the training dynamics of DL and leverage gained insight into a wide range of applications covering affective computing, health, and industry applications. With my main focus being on audio, I have experience in general signal processing, as well as computer vision, and natural language processing.

In 2025, I was awarded my PhD (Dr. rer. nat.) with Summa Cum Laude (highest honour) from TUM's School of [Computation, Information and Technology](https://www.cit.tum.de/en/cit/home/). Prior to that, I completed my M.Sc. in Physics (2018), as  well as my B.Sc. in Informatics (2015) and my B.Sc. in Physics (2014) in the [University of Augsburg](https://www.uni-augsburg.de/en/).

Besides research, I take a lot of joy in teaching, including supervision of bachelor and master theses - or all other kinds of guided research, as well as structured courses. I am actively gathering teaching experience since my 2nd semester at university (after already being a tutor for fellow pupils at high school since the age of ~13), creating content for and giving tutorials and lectures. I have also been actively shaping and coordinating the general teaching curriculum of our research group since 2019. 

Besides my current research group, I have been part of 
- Chair of Embedded Intelligence for Health Care (EIHW) and Wellbeing @ University of Augsburg, led by Prof. Björn Schuller (employed research assistant 2018-2023)
- [Group on Language, Audio, and Music](https://glam.doc.ic.ac.uk/) @ [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/), led by Prof. Björn Schuller (short term visitor, 2024)
- [Human-in-the-Loop Computing Group](https://wp.doc.ic.ac.uk/bkainz/) @ Imperial College London, led by Prof. Dr. [Bernhard Kainz](https://bernhard-kainz.com/) (short term visitor, 2025)
